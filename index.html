Gangle, I didn't think I was going to like her as much as I do, but for some reason I find Gangle to be the most relatable character in the show.

I found her kind of interesting, but she didn't really stick out to me because of her short amount of screentime and because of how weak and a crybaby she is, but I unfortunately came to the conclusion that if I was anyone in this wonderful show then I would probably be her.

For those who don't know, Gangle is one of the main characters of the show "The Amazing Digital Circus" on Youtube. A show that really goes into the depth and mentality of each prisoner in the digital penetentiary.

Now for the reasons I relate to Gangle in extremely neccessary bullet points.

1. She is an artist and likes drawing anime or cartoons.

2. Using her masks, she presents herself as a happy comical person, but the mask can easily break into a depressed and low energy version of herself.

3. We can both be pretty silly and crazy when nothing stops us.

4. We both struggle a little with loving ourselves from time to time.

5. She can get a little bossy and perfectionist when in a position of power.

6. Can eventually become happier with the help of close friends.
